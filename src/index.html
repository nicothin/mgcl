About

Magical star international co.ltd. - our main goal is the satisfaction of our customers. We have been a leader in the paintball and airsoft community for over 5 years and have helped grow the sport into what it is today. We strive to provide you with a pleasant and easy purchase experience by offering the best price, best service, best selection, promptness, and reliability.

Magical co. ltd is manufacturing and distribution company of paintball sporting goods. Dedicated to quality, craftsmanship, and customer service it embodies the sport of Paintball and Airsoft - Life, Function, Style.

We provide with field equipment such as paintball markers, tanks, safety goggles, airsoft guns, bb’s, gas, repair parts, HP compressors etс.

All of the products are properly certified in countries they produced.

We are guarantee to our clients high quality service as well as our good staff and competitive terms.

We have a special price for the products of our partners. Accordingly, and in turn we can offer goods at very competitive prices and significantly lower if you contact the manufacturer.
